Butter Chicken


Description

Ingredients

For the Chicken and Marinade:

  • 500g Boneless Chicken Thighs, cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 3 tbsp Plain Greek Yogurt or thick curd
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder (gives the signature red color without intense heat)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice & 1 tsp Salt

For Makhni Gravy:

  • 4 tbsp Butter & 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 Large Onion, finely sliced
  • 4 Large Tomatoes, chopped (or 1 cup tomato puree)
  • 10-12 Whole Raw Cashews (essential for a rich, creamy texture)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder & 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • ½ cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 tsp Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crushed
  • 1 tsp Sugar or honey to balance acidity

Steps

Marinate The Chicken

  1. Combine Ingredients:Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, Kashmiri chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Coat Chicken:Add chicken chunks and toss until evenly coated.
  3. Rest: Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or ideally overnight for maximum tenderness.

Sear the Chicken

  1. Heat Pan: Melt 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Brown Meat:Brown meat: Add chicken pieces in a single layer. Do not overcrowd the pan.
  3. Cook: Sear for 3–4 minutes per side until beautifully browned and slightly charred. Remove and set aside (it will finish cooking in the sauce).

Build the Gravy Base

  1. Saute aromatics:In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of butter. Sauté sliced onions until soft and translucent.
  2. Add Base:Stir in ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomatoes, and raw cashews.
  3. Simmer Add a splash of water, cover, and let cook for 10 minutes until tomatoes turn completely soft and mushy.
  4. Blend Remove from heat, let it cool slightly, and blend the mixture until perfectly smooth. Tip: Strain the blended paste through a sieve back into the pan for that ultra-velvety restaurant texture.

Combine and Finish

  1. Simmer Sauce:Pour the smooth sauce back into the pan over medium-low heat. Stir in the remaining cumin and Kashmiri chili powder.Pour the smooth sauce back into the pan over medium-low heat. Stir in the remaining cumin and Kashmiri chili powder.
  2. Incorporate Chicken Add the seared chicken chunks and any resting juices back into the bubbling sauce. Simmer for 8–10 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Add the "Butter":Stir in the remaining butter, sugar/honey, and heavy cream. Let it cook on low heat for 2 minutes.
  4. Final TouchFinal touch: Rub the kasuri methi between your palms to release its aroma and sprinkle it over the curry. Turn off the heat.

Serve hot garnished with a drizzle of cream and fresh coriander, alongside warm garlic naan or steamed basmati rice.
