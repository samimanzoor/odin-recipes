Pizza


Description

Ingredients

  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Flour
  • Warm Water
  • Olive OIl
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Activate the Yeast: In a large bowl (or stand mixer), combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5–7 minutes until it becomes frothy and bubbly.
  2. Make the Dough:Add the flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix until a soft, slightly tacky dough forms.
  3. Knead:Knead for 5 minutes by hand (or 2 minutes in a stand mixer) until the dough is smooth and springy. Knead for 5 minutes by hand (or 2 minutes in a stand mixer) until the dough is smooth and springy.
  4. ProofLightly oil a large bowl. Place your dough inside, turning it to coat. Cover the bowl with a damp towel or plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm spot for \(45\) to \(60\) minutes, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. PreheatWhile the dough rises, preheat your oven to \(240^{\circ }C\) (\(475^{\circ }F\)). If using a pizza stone, leave it inside to heat up.
  6. Shape the DoughPunch down the dough and transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Use your hands or a rolling pin to stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  7. AssembleTransfer the dough to a greased pizza pan or parchment paper. Brush the edges with olive oil, spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base, and top with mozzarella and your choice of toppings.
  8. BakeBake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly charred
